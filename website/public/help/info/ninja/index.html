NINJA NINJAS

April 28, 2026

The ninja is a member of the oriental society of mercenaries; A master of the ancient secret stealth combat style called ninjitsu. They have abilities that no other class can offer. Ninja have no remort class. Because of the stealthy style of their combat, Ninjas are barred from using shields of any sort. Ninjas may only be human.
