ASSASSIN ASSASSINS

April 28, 2026

Assassins are cold and deadly killers for hire, whose knowledge of subterfuge, stealth and the Black Arts makes them extremely efficient, extremely deadly opponents. Assassins are found worldwide, although they are usually concentrated in cities. Because of the nature of their work, the assassin class is barred from using shields of any kind. Because of the skill, knowledge, and experience that is required to be an assassin, this class is remort only.
