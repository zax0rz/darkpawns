AVATAR AVATARS

April 28, 2026

Avatars are the emissaries of the Holy Forces in the land, deriving their awesome power from those entities. Although they can heal and protect like clerics, the reason that the Avatars exist is to strike down the Unholy Forces in the land, and their masters have given them powerful magicks to aid them to this end. Because of the intensity of belief required to be an avatar, this class is remort only.
