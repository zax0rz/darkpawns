CLAN CLANS

April 28, 2026

On Dark Pawns, CLANS are associations of players that have banded together for a specific purpose. Most CLANS are made up of adventurers who have decided to work together for mutual gain.

If you would like to form your own clan, you need to be over level 10 and enlist the aid of two other players (not multis) who are currently not in any other clans. After these requirements have been met, ask to speak to a God. Additionally, there is usually a minor quest or task involved in order to finalize the creation of your new clan.

CLAN WHO Displays the names and ranks of fellow clan-members that are currently online.

CLAN MEMBERS Displays all the members in your clan.

CLAN STATUS Displays your current rank in your CLAN.

CLAN INFO Displays all available information on a named CLAN (type CLAN INFO without any arguments to get a list of current CLANS).

CLAN QUIT Allows you to leave your clan behind.

CLAN APPLY Apply to join a named clan, but only if you meet their requirements.

CLAN ENROLL Allows you to enroll players into your clan. Typing CLAN ENROLL without any arguments shows a list of players who have applied to join your clan.

CLAN EXPEL Allows you to kick someone out of your clan.

CLAN PROMOTE Promote a player in your clan to a higher rank

CLAN DEMOTE Demote a player in your clan to a lower rank

CLAN WITHDRAW Make a withdrawal from the clan bank account

CLAN DEPOSIT Deposit into the clan bank account

CLAN SET RANKS Set the number of ranks in your clan

CLAN SET APPFEE Set the application fee for people to apply for your clan

CLAN SET DUES Set the required monthly dues for your clan members

CLAN SET APPLEV Set the minimum level a player must be to apply to your clan

CLAN SET PLAN Create a description of your clan for all to see

CLAN PRIVATE Toggle whether or not your clan is private or public

CLAN SET PRIVILEGE Set the privileges of the clan commands for clan members

CLAN SET TITLE Set the title for each rank in your clan

See also: CTELL
