REPLY .

April 28, 2026

Usage: reply or .

This command will send a reply to the last person to send you a message using TELL. It works just as if you had typed ’tell ' but makes it a bit easier. It can also be very useful in case someone invisible sends you a message.

Example:

Ras tells you, ‘Hi!’

reply hey You tell Ras, ‘hey’

Someone tells you, ‘I am your secret admirer.’

. Who are you? You tell someone, ‘Who are you?’

See also: TELL, NOTELL
