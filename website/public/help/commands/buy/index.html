BUY

April 28, 2026

Usage: buy buy # buy n. buy n buy n #

If you’d like to purchase an item from a shop and have the money, you can buy it. Items can be bought by name, or by their number in the shop listing, and can be bought in quantity.

Examples:

buy sword (buy the first sword on the list) buy #3 (buy the third item on the list) buy 4.sword (buy the fourth sword on the list) buy 10 bread (buy 10 pieces of bread) buy 10 #2 (buy 10 of the second item on the list)

See also: LIST, SELL, SHOPS, VALUE
