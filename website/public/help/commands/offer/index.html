OFFER RENT

April 28, 2026

Usage: offer rent

RENT has been disabled. See QUIT. If you have an item that is !RENT, it will disappear when you quit.

RENT files time out with inactivity. Normal rent files (where you QUIT normally) will timeout and be deleted after 30 real life days of inactivity. This means that if you do not log into the mud every month, your items will be deleted. In the event of a crash save or idle save, the timeout period is 10 days. If for some reason the mud goes down for more than 10 days, these restrictions may be lifted.
