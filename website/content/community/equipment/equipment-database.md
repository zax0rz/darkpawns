# equipment*


---


This is the equipment section of the page. By no means is this meant to be a complete
   list of the extensive equipment available on Dark Pawns, but it is a reference for some
   of the best items you can attain, as well as "basic" items. Also available are racial
   weapons, the list of battleground items    you need to make SBA, and much more. I've
   identified the equipment so you don't have to. If you would    like to contribute an
   item's statistics that I've forgotten, please don't hesitate to send the webmaster an
   [email](https://web.archive.org/web/20040717141615/mailto:aidan@dp-players.com).
