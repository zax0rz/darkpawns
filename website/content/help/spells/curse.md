Usage       : cast 'curse' <victim | object>
Accumulative: Yes
Duration    : Several hours, permanent on objects.

Curse cast on a person that fails the saving throw, will reduce their
hitroll and damage roll significantly.  It will also reduce the saving
throw versus paralysis by one. At higher levels, it is possible that
a curse might cause the victim to have an unfortunate accident of the
lethal type.

When cast upon an object, the object will become undropable.
