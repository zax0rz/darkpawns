Usage: cast 'gate'

This spell, when cast in the secluded groves and magickal centers of energy
where moongates normally appear, causes a portal to appear, regardless of
the date or time. 

This portal functions similar to other moongates; taking you to the farthest
reaches of the cosmos where the moongates intersect in their
faster-than-light paths of travel.

Casting this spell in a room where a gate already exists can have strange,
far-reaching side-affects. Few have returned to tell what those side-affects 
actually are.
