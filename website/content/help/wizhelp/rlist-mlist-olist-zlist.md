Usage: rlist <zone-number>
       mlist <zone-number>
       olist <zone-number>
       zlist <zone number>

Lists the rooms in a zone (rlist), objects in a zone (olist), mobs in a 
zone (mlist), or zone-commands in a zone (zlist).
