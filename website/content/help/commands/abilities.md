Usage: abilities

This command will display your current ability scores in the following
format:

   Your current ability scores: 
    Strength:      (excellent)
    Dexterity:     (decent)
    Intelligence:  (decent)
    Wisdom:        (fair)
    Constitution:  (good)
    Charisma:      (bad)

There are not any numeric representations of ability scores on Dark Pawns.
