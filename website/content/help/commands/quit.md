When you get tired of playing, you can leave the game by typing 'quit'.
There is no RENT on Dark Pawns. Your equipment is saved as long as you 
quit out in a safe area.  To see a list of how long your equipment is 
saved for, type HELP RENT.  

Again, to keep your items, you must quit in the safe area in your hometown. 
The safe areas are the Temple of the Unholy in Kir Drax'in, The public temple 
in Kir-Oshi, and the ziggurat in Alaozar.

If you have to type REALLYQUIT to leave the game, your items did not save,
and were dropped on the ground in the room you quit out in.

See Also: HOMETOWN, RECALL, WORD OF RECALL, RENT, REALLYQUIT
