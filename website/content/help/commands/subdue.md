Usage: subdue <victim>

When you don't want to kill them (right away), subdue them. This command,
When executed successfully, leaves the victim stunned and unable to move 
until the next tick of the game clock. A successful subdue maneuver leaves
the victim unknowing who subdued them.

See also: THIEF, NINJA, ASSASSIN
