Usage: peek <victim>

When being caught staring could be bad for your health, peek lets you size
up your victim without being noticed. A failed peek is just like a normal
"look <victim>"; however it's hard to tell if you're noticed. (Unless of 
course, they start pounding on you.)

See also: THIEF, ASSASSIN
