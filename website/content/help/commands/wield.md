Usage: wield <weapon>

When you get tired of bashing monsters with your fists, you might consider
wielding a weapon.

Example:

  > wield sword
  > wield 3.sword    (Wield the third sword in your inventory)

See also: EQUIPMENT, REMOVE, WEAR
