The legendary sleeper hold. Just grab your opponent by the neck and soon
its sleepy time for them. Zzzzz.

Note: sleeper works best on humanoid-types and some people may be too big
or too strong to knock unconscious using this method. Also, you must be 
bare-handed to sleeper someone.

See also: WARRIOR, PALADIN, RANGER
