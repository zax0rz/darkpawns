Usage: remove <item>
       remove all
       remove all.<keyword>

If you tire of wearing some armor or wielding a weapon, remove it.

Examples:

  > remove boots
  > remove sword
  > remove all
  > remove all.bronze

See also: GRAB, HOLD, WEAR, WIELD
