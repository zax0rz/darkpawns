Commands for changing your position.  If you feel weary, it's probably 
a good idea to sit and rest for a while, or maybe even take a nap.

The SLEEP command puts you to sleep, allowing you to regain your health 
More quickly.  You will be largely unaware of your surroundings while 
asleep. You can awaken yourself (and others) with the WAKE command.

Also, certain rooms such as holy rooms and places of ancient power allow
you to heal and regenerate faster.
