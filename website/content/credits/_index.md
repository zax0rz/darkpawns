# Credits

## Original Staff (1997–2010)

Dark Pawns is built on CircleMUD, itself derived from DikuMUD. The lineage: DikuMUD → CircleMUD 3.0 → Dark Pawns.

### Creator

- **Serapis** (Derek Karnes) — Original creator and implementor. Founded Dark Pawns in September 1994 on CircleMUD 1.7.

### Implementors

- **Orodreth** (S. Thompson) — Joined February 1996. Co-implementor from October 1997. Primary maintainer through the Wolfpaw era and beyond. Original author of the DP history document (circa 1999).
- **Frontline** (R.E. Paret) — Joined March 1996. Co-implementor from May 1998. Maintained the Dark Pawns website, recorded the community history, and sustained the project through its later years.

### Developers & Builders

- **Tracer** (C. Jackson) — Co-developer with Serapis in the earliest days (1994).
- **Arak** (B. Parker) — Coder, joined February 1996.
- **Oddity** (A. McConnell) — Head builder, joined February 1996. Left October 1997.
- **Nimos** — Builder, joined March 1996.
- **Aralius** (R. Martin) — Builder, joined March 1996.
- **Doragar** — Builder/coder, joined December 1999.
- **Delirium** — Immortal staff, joined December 1998. Demoted October 1999.
- **Hulk** — Immortal staff, joined December 1998. Demoted October 1999.

### Hosting

- **Mystech** — Serapis' employer, hosted DP on `pawns.guru.org` (1997).
- **Wolfpaw.net** — Free MUD hosting provider, hosted DP from December 1998 through the paid era.

## Resurrection (2025–Present)

- **Zach Greene** (zax0rz) — Go server port, modern infrastructure, and project lead for the 2025 revival.
- Open source on [GitHub](https://github.com/zax0rz/dark-pawns).
- Modern stack: Go server, Hugo website, WebSocket web client.

## Community

Dark Pawns was played by hundreds of people over more than a decade. The player roster preserved on [dp-players.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20040712053500/http://www.dp-players.com/) represents a snapshot from June 2004 — one moment in a long-running community. Everyone who rolled a character, joined a clan, or logged in at 3 AM to grind levels contributed to what Dark Pawns was.

The [player roster](/credits/player-roster/) and [wizard list](/credits/wizlist/) are preserved from original site archives.
