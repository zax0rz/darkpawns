## CRITICAL

### CRIT-001: PROTECT_EVIL and PROTECT_GOOD don't execute raw_kill on alignment violation

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_affects()` — if `IS_EVIL(victim)` when casting PROT_FROM_EVIL (or `IS_GOOD(victim)` for PROTECT_GOOD), C calls `raw_kill(ch, TYPE_BLAST)` which **kills the caster** and logs the death
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAffects()` — just sends a "you cannot protect yourself" message and returns
- **Divergence:** Missing lethal consequence for alignment-based self-targeting
- **Impact:** Evil characters can freely receive protection from evil without dying. This removes a significant risk/reward mechanic that's been in the game for 30 years

### CRIT-002: HELLFIRE level 4 skip reversed — C kills low-level, Go skips them

- **C source:** `spells.c:spell_hellfire()` — characters with `GET_LEVEL <= 4` get `damage(ch, tmp_victim, GET_MAX_HIT(tmp_victim)*12, SPELL_HELLFIRE)` which is instant death (12x max hp)
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:castHellfire()` — characters with `cn.GetLevel() <= 4` are skipped with `continue`
- **Divergence:** The condition is inverted — C deals massive instant-kill damage to low-level chars, Go makes them immune
- **Impact:** Low-level characters standing in hellfire become immune instead of instantly dying. Completely opposite behavior from the C original

### CRIT-003: FLAMESTRIKE is a DOT in C, direct damage in Go

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_affects()` — flamestrike is registered with MAG_AFFECTS routine. It applies AFF_FLAMING (a damage-over-time affect), has `!OUTSIDE(ch)` restriction, saving throw, and sets a DOT with `duration = GET_LEVEL(ch)*0.17`
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/spell_info.go:init()` — flamestrike registered as `RoutineDamage`. `damage_spells.go:MagDamage()` deals `dam = dice(12,8) + level*2` as direct burst damage
- **Divergence:** C applies Flaming as a burn DOT that deals damage over time. Go deals direct burst damage
- **Impact:** Flamestrike becomes a direct damage spell instead of a damage-over-time effect. No outdoors restriction, no saving throw check, no AFF_FLAMING flag set. Completely different spell behavior

## HIGH

### HIGH-001: CallMagic castType savetype mapping diverges from C

- **C source:** `spell_parser.c:call_magic()` — CAST_STAFF→SAVING_ROD, CAST_SCROLL→SAVING_ROD, CAST_POTION→SAVING_ROD, CAST_WAND→SAVING_ROD. CAST_SPELL→SAVING_SPELL. default→SAVING_BREATH
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/call_magic.go:CallMagic()` — CastWand→SaveParalysis, CastStaff→SaveBreath, CastScroll→SaveSpell, CastPotion→SaveParalysis, default→SaveSpell
- **Divergence:** Every cast type uses a different save type
- **Impact:** Every magic item in the game (wands, staves, scrolls, potions) uses the wrong saving throw type. Wands save vs Paralysis (C: Rod), Scrolls save vs Spell (C: Rod), Potions save vs Paralysis (C: Rod), Staves save vs Breath (C: Rod)

### HIGH-002: CHARM duration formula different — C uses GET_INT(victim), Go applies no affect

- **C source:** `spells.c:spell_charm()` — duration = `24 * 18 / GET_INT(victim)` (zero-guard: `24 * 18`). Applies AFF_CHARM via affect_to_char() with duration, modifier, location
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:castCharm()` — no affect struct applied to victim. Just stops existing follower and re-adds via AddFollowerQuiet(). No duration, no AFF_CHARM flag
- **Divergence:** Charm duration and AFF_CHARM flag are missing
- **Impact:** Charmed mobs never break free from charm (no duration tick). No AFF_CHARM flag means charm-specific code paths check wrong state

### HIGH-003: METALSKIN AC modifier missing reagent bonus

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_affects()` — AC modifier = `-(15 + GET_LEVEL(ch)/2 + reag)` — includes reagent bonus
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAffects()` — AC modifier = `-(15 + getLevel(ch)/2)` — no reagent bonus
- **Divergence:** Reagent bonus not applied to AC
- **Impact:** Mages with the iron chunk reagent don't get extra AC from metalskin. Lost ~10-25 AC at high levels

### HIGH-004: METEOR SWARM damage range upper bound off by 10

- **C source:** `spells.c:spell_meteor_swarm()` — dam = `GET_LEVEL(ch)*6 + number(-10, GET_LEVEL(ch)*3)` = range: [level*6-10, level*6+level*3]
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:castMeteorSwarm()` — dam = `level*6 + rand.Intn(level*3+1) - 10` = range: [level*6-10, level*6+level*3-10]
- **Divergence:** rand.Intn(n) returns [0,n), so max is level*3-10 instead of level*3
- **Impact:** Maximum meteor swarm damage is 10 points lower than the C original at all levels

## MEDIUM

### MEDIUM-001: SLEEP spell missing PvP restrictions and reagent from C

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_affects()` — Outlaw-only PvP check, +/-3 level range check, MOB_NOSLEEP check, reagent (sand) adds duration, sets victim position to POS_SLEEPING
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAffects()` — just applies sleep affect. No PvP restriction, no level range, no reagent, no position change
- **Divergence:** Multiple C behaviors missing
- **Impact:** Sleep works cross-level against all PCs regardless of outlaw status, no reagent consumption, no position change

### MEDIUM-002: HELLFIRE damage formula completely different

- **C source:** `spells.c:spell_hellfire()` — `dice(12, 5) + (2*level) - 10` (avg: ~26 + 2*level)
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/damage_spells.go:MagDamage()` — `dice(level/5+1, 12) + (2*level) - 10`
- **Divergence:** C uses fixed dice(12,5); Go scales the number of dice with level
- **Impact:** Different damage curve at all levels. Level 50: C avg ~126, Go avg ~156. Level 10: C avg ~46, Go avg ~33

### MEDIUM-003: HEAL and MASS HEAL cutthroat affect removal omitted

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_points()` — heal/mass_heal check `affected_by_spell(victim, SKILL_CUTTHROAT)` and call `affect_from_char(victim, SKILL_CUTTHROAT)`
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagPoints()` — no cutthroat affect removal in heal path
- **Divergence:** Missing cutthroat healing mechanic
- **Impact:** Cutthroat skill effect (preventing natural healing) persists through healing spells

### MEDIUM-004: Cure light/critic/heal room messages missing

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_points()` — sends `act("$N's wounds glow and mend themselves."...)` to room for cure_light, cure_critic, heal, mass_heal (when caster != victim)
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagPoints()` — no room messages sent for healing
- **Divergence:** Missing visual feedback
- **Impact:** Players don't see when others cast healing spells

### MEDIUM-005: MagAreas has no to_room action messages for area spells

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_areas()` — sends both to_char and to_room messages for earthquake ("$n gracefully gestures and the earth begins to shake violently!") and acid_blast
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAreas()` — only sends to_char messages, no to_room act() calls
- **Divergence:** Missing room-wide messages
- **Impact:** Room doesn't show earthquake/acid blast animation

### MEDIUM-006: MINDSIGHT missing AFF_NOTRACK / notrack check

- **C source:** `spells.c:spell_mindsight()` — checks `IS_AFFECTED(victim, AFF_NOTRACK)` and blocks with "Cannot find the target of your will!"
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:castMindsight()` — no notrack check
- **Divergence:** Missing protection bypass
- **Impact:** Mindsight bypasses notrack protection

## LOW

### LOW-001: CURSE weapon damage reduction not implemented

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_alter_objs()` — curse on weapon: `GET_OBJ_VAL(obj, 2)--` reduces weapon damage dice
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAlterObjs()` — curse just sets NODROP, no weapon damage reduction
- **Divergence:** Missing weapon cursing mechanic

### LOW-002: REMOVE_CURSE weapon damage restoration not implemented — uses wrong flag

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_alter_objs()` — remove curse on weapon: `GET_OBJ_VAL(obj, 2)++` restores the damage die. Also: `REMOVE_BIT_AR(GET_OBJ_EXTRA(obj), ITEM_NODROP)`
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAlterObjs()` — remove curse for weapons: sets ITEM_BLESS flag instead of clearing NODROP. The Go version sets the wrong flag entirely

### LOW-003: mag_alter_objs REMOVE_POISON case missing in Go

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_alter_objs()` — REMOVE_POISON clears poison on drink containers/food (sets values[3]=0)
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAlterObjs()` — no REMOVE_POISON case in object alteration

### LOW-004: POISON affect missing stat penalties

- **C source:** `magic.c:mag_affects()` — poison applies APPLY_STR (-2) AND APPLY_HITROLL (-2) in addition to AFF_POISON bitvector
- **Go port:** `pkg/spells/affect_spells.go:MagAffects()` — poison applies bitvector only, no stat mods
