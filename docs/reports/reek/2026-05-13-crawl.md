**cmdReload — %s in broadcast with no format args**
file: pkg/session/wiz_system.go:70

`"\\r\\n*** World data reload initiated by %s. ***\\r\\n"` sent to SendToAll. no format arguments. the `%s` literal appears in every player's broadcast. lines 76 and 83 have the same `\\r\\n` pattern but at least no broken format specifier.

**cmdFlee — XP loss can invert when mob healed above max HP**
file: pkg/session/combat_cmds.go:~78

`loss := opponent.GetMaxHP() - opponent.GetHP()` — if a mob was healed above its max HP (rare but possible through room resets or script heals), this produces negative loss. multiplied by level and added to XP formula, the player _gains_ XP for fleeing.

why it matters: edge case combat exploit. flee XP gain on unusual mob interactions.

suggested fix: `max(0, opponent.GetMaxHP() - opponent.GetHP())`
